# 🌙 Islami App

Islami is a mobile application designed to help users stay connected with their daily Islamic practices through a simple and user-friendly experience. The app provides essential tools such as Quran reading, Tasbeeh, and Islamic content in one place.

---

## 📌 Problem Statement

Many users find it difficult to maintain consistency in their daily Islamic habits due to the lack of simple and organized tools. Switching between multiple apps can be inconvenient and distracting.

---

## 💡 Solution

Islami brings all essential Islamic features into one application, allowing users to easily access Quran content, perform daily Tasbeeh, and stay spiritually connected in a smooth and distraction-free experience.

---

## ✨ Key Features

* 📖 Quran reading
* 🔢 Digital Tasbeeh counter
* 🌙 Islamic content in one place
* 📱 Clean and simple UI
* ⚡ Easy navigation and smooth experience

---

## 🧱 Architecture

The project follows a structured and scalable architecture:
